Down for Everyone or Just Me

Short URL @   downfor.io

 down for everyone 
   
This site checks if a website is down for everyone or just you.

For example, if you can’t load or login to Facebook you can check if Facebook is down here and we will tell you if it is down when our servers check. This can be handy to know if the problem is with everyone or just something with your ISP or local area.
